Welcome

These spots are certainly a must go. They have an atmosphere that is uncomparable to
anything you have ever expereienced. They are authentic, delicious and hip. You will be
dining with the best and being served by the best. The food is highlighted all over the
media because everyome agrees it is the most amazing, beating out all of its competition by far
Miami is known for its fun vibe and certainly wide array of food. Take a weekend to experience
food from all over the world by staying in one area. The restaurants highlighted on this site are
Roberta's Pizza, Nusr-Et steakhouse, The Eating
House, Macchailina.

If you are looking for a more relaxed atmosphere, Eating House, Robert'as Pizza and Juanchi's
are all super casual and almost have a bar atmosphere. You can totally get away with wearing
jeans, which in Miami, is not very common. Just becasue they are casual does not mean their food
is anything boring. They still offer amazing dishes but you do not need to stress about what you are
wearing when you enjoy thir food.

Nusr-Et and Macchailina are a little more nice. They definitely give off a more Miami type of
vibe and I would get a little more done up to attend these places. They food is extravagant and the
crowd that goes to these spots definitely know what good food is. They still offer a bar with a
cool interior and you will not see chandalers or white couches, but you will see a modern feel
with random antique looking decorations.
