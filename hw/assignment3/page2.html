Nusr-Et Steakhouse

You heard it correct ladies and gentleman...Salt Bae has landed in Brickell.
The extravagant menu offers a meat based variety and serves up one of the best
experiences you have ever witnessed at a resturaunt. Make sure to plan about a
month in advance beacuse a reservation to this place does not come by easy.

Eating House Miami

Bring back your childhood memories at Eating House Miami as they offer Cap'N Crunch
Pancakes and dirt cups! This place definitely has a vibe of south beach but it is
located in Coral Gables. It is fun, it is hip and it is delicious. They have a variety
of foods and as much as they like to branch out, they have classic dishes that everyone
loves. The brunch is a must but the dinner is definitely worth the taste as well.

Macchailina

Forget the Cuban food and the New York pizza...Macchailina offers classic Rustic Italian food
that has been a hit since June 2012. They have been featured in almost every magazine Miami
has to offer and they are always busy. This spot is a cozy area located right off of Alton Road.
They do amazing seafood dishes and traditional Spaghetti and Meatballs. Everything is fresh and homeade
and definitely worth it to give it a try.

Juanchi's Burger

Miami has finally gotten what it has been searching for....BURGERS. Real, juicy, decorated, burgers.
Handcrafted buns and beef is just the beginning to these burgers. They have onions, mushrooms, arugula,
chorizo and tostones added in between the buns as well. These burgers are no joke and niether are
the owners behind this place. They know what they are doing so allow your cheat day to be spent at
Juanchi's and eat a burger while you sip on there famous pina colodas.
