City of Brotherly Love

‹ › ×

    When you touch down in the city of brotherly love a bunch of different things rush into your mind. Whether it is food, music or sight seeing, Philly has it all to offer. For the classic people who want some history, the Liberty Bell is a great thing to check out. If you want a little more picture taking moments, stop by the Art Museum and get a picture with the Rocky statue. Once history class is over, Head to Lincoln Financial field and soak up the feeling of victory and smell the Super Bowl win all around the stadium. Hungry? Philly is known for its cheesteaks of course. Take a pit stop and try the two most famous spots. Be the judge if Pat's or Geno's is better. Not full? No worries, take a cab to the Reading terminal market and indulge in classic Philadelphia drinks and meals. Top your summer off with the music festival of the summer. Located right in front of the Art Museuem, enjoy music of all types on Labor Day Weekend.
