Philly Phavorites

Food, Music and sight seeing is a basic way to look at Philly. The intense battle of who has the best cheesteak is a true debate. Along with that, Philly is known for its Reading Terminal Market filled with Philly classics. If food really isnt your thing, take a historic trip to the Liberty bell or go hangout with the Rocky statue at the Art Mueseum. Come from a losing city? Take a tour through Lincoln Financial Field and experience the feeling of superbowl champs.

    1. If you are into football or not, The Linc is a great spot to relax and relive history. Being Superbowl champs has led them to get a bunch of new features that guests like to see.The area also offers The Xfinity center to get food and drinks pre or post game.
    2. History lover? Go see the Liberty Bell in center city located on Market street. You can also see the surrounding museums and resturaunts located near the bell.
    3. Get a great last weekend of summer in the books and spend it at the Made in America Music festival. Located right on the Ben Franklin Parkway, overlooking the art musuem. They always have a line up that contains all the genres that would please whoever you would be going with.
    4. Welcome to the Reading Terminal Market. One of America’s largest and oldest public markets, housed since 1893 in a National Historic Landmark building, the Market offers an incredible selection of locally grown & exotic produce, locally sourced meats and poultry, plus the finest seafood, cheeses, baked goods, and confections.
    5. The infamous Rocky Balboa statue is located in the heart of the Ben Franklin Parkway. He stands below the art museum which makes it a perfect reason to stop and get a picture.
    6. Cheesteaks. Philly's favorite all day meal. Anywhere you go within 20 miles of the city offers their version of the cheesteak. Whether its different bread, cheese, or toppings, there is no doubt that a cheesteak is what the city of known for. For decades, two resturaunts have been competing over which has the best sandwhich. I guess you are going to have to travel to both and make the decsion.
